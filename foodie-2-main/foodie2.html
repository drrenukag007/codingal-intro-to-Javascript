Imran Ijaz

Imran Ijaz (CEO, Cheezious) on receiving the CEO Award from Dr. I Wayan Serinah (Governor of Bali Special Assistant for Economic and Development Affairs) at CEO Summit Bali by World CEO Forum & CEO Club Pakistan at Truntum Kuta, Bali Indonesia.

Cheezious has marked its presence as one of the major fast-food brands. With the pride of being Pakistani at heart, it continues to compete at par with multinational brands that have been established and present for decades.

Cheezious is dedicated to deepening its connection with the local community, positioning itself as a culinary landmark that reflects the vibrant culture and passion for food in the city. With its modern facade and upbeat ambiance, it aims to redefine the dining experience.
